Assessment and Contract Development

You can access forms and materials related to the assessment of your skills from this menu. Specifically, you can take or review your results on the Foundational Knowledge Quiz. You can access the Supervisee Self-Assessment which you take at the beginning, during, and at the conclusion of your supervisory experience to evaluate your ability to perform tasks/skills. You can also browse through the Job Models your supervisor posted which summarizes the tasks and skills you will be trained in. Finally, you can view the goal Supervision Feedback and Goal Setting Form which helps you and your supervisor pinpoint specific skills areas for supervision.

Training Development

Your supervisor continuously develops tools and templates helping him/her to provide you with the highest quality supervision and enhance your experience as a supervisee. Your supervisor may sometimes choose to share those materials with you for your viewing. The main tools you may have access to are task analyses and training storyboards.
